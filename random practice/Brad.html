Walk in Confidence and Freedom

Evangelistic Message

Brad’s signature talk helps men step into true confidence and freedom. He challenges them to lay down the junk that’s holding them back, discover their identity in Christ, and live with boldness and humility. Drawing from his NFL experience and personal story, Brad connects with students, athletes, and men at all stages of life.

“Let us also lay aside every weight, and sin which clings so closely, and let us run with endurance the race that is set before us, looking to Jesus, the founder and perfecter of our faith…” — Hebrews 12:1–2
